Language Used : MySQL

Problem Statement:
Query the NAME field for all American cities in the CITY table with populations larger than 120000.
The CountryCode for America is USA.

Solution:
SELECT NAME
FROM CITY 
WHERE COUNTRYCODE = "USA" AND POPULATION > 120000

Explanation:
Here we are Selecting all the attributes(columns) from the city table and
then filtering out by countrycode and population, which are attributes of the City table.
